Good to Know

Questions, answered.

Everything you might want to know before booking. Still unsure about something? Just message us.

Pricing depends on size, detail and placement, so there's no flat rate for custom work. Smaller fine line pieces start lower, while large detailed work like realism or full sleeves is quoted per project. Share your idea with us and we'll give you an honest quote. Consultations are free, with no pressure to commit.

We're appointment-only. That way every client gets a fully prepared station, dedicated time and our full attention, instead of being rushed. Message us on WhatsApp or Instagram to book a slot.

Realism, black & grey, colour, pet portraits, religious and deity work, and fine line — all custom-designed for you. We work across other styles too, but these are where we're strongest.

Always, and we go further than most. It isn't just the needles. Everything used or touched during setup is either single-use and disposable, or wrapped with a fresh barrier for every client. From the moment your station is prepared to the moment you leave, hygiene is treated as non-negotiable.

There's some discomfort, but most clients are surprised by how manageable it is. Pain varies by placement — areas with more flesh hurt less than bony areas. We work at a comfortable pace and talk you through it, especially if it's your first.

Three simple things make a big difference: get a good night's sleep, eat a heavy breakfast or proper meal before you arrive, and if possible, moisturise the area you're getting tattooed in the days before. Avoid alcohol for 24 hours before, and wear comfortable clothing that gives easy access to the area. We'll guide you through the rest on the day.

Surface healing usually takes about two weeks, with full healing over a month or two. We'll give you clear aftercare guidance after your session so it heals cleanly and the work stays sharp.

We're in Naupada, Thane West — easy to reach from across Thane and the wider Mumbai metro. Once your appointment is confirmed, we'll share the exact address and directions.

We accept cash and UPI. Card payments aren't available at this time.

There are a few ways. The fastest is to call or WhatsApp us with your idea, rough size, placement and budget. If you'd rather explain everything in detail first, fill out our booking form and our team will call you back to confirm details and book your slot. You can also DM us on Instagram.
